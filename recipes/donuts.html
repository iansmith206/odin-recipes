Homemade Donuts

A tasty Dutch treat

A donut is a kind of fried dough confectionary. Donuts are typically ring shaped with a hole in the middle. Bakers usually make oval shaped donuts, which are often filled with custards or fruit preserves.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Water
  • Frosting

Steps

      Mix eggs, milk, and water in a bowl
      Place in fridge and let it sit for 30 minutes
      Cut into small balls and press them flat, then punch hole in center
      Place in bowl of oil at 350 degrees and fry for 60 seconds
