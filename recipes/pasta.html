Homemade Pasta

Italian comfort food

Pasta is a type of food made from a mixture of flour, eggs and water that is formed into different shapes and then boiled. Spaghetti, macaroni, and noodles are types of pasta.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Water

Steps

  1. Add flour, eggs, salt and water in a bowl
  2. Knead dough for 10 minutes and wrap in plastic
  3. Place in refridgerator for 30 minutes
  4. Roll dough and slice into thin sheets
  5. Roll through pasta roller three times until thin pasts in finished
  6. Cook for 2 minutes and add sauce
